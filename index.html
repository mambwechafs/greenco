About Us

Welcome to Greenstretch Consultants Ltd, a fully independent environmental consultancy and circular economy company based in Zambia. Founded in early 2018, we take pride in our dedicated and multi-disciplinary team of well-trained professionals. Our expertise allows us to successfully handle a variety of tasks and projects from start to finish, ensuring compliance with regulations and sustainability within required timelines.

  • Comprehensive Environmental Consultancy Services
  • Sustainable Waste Management Solutions
  • Health and Safety Services

Our mission is to provide top-quality environmental consultancy and sustainable waste management solutions to enhance the quality of life and the environment in Zambia. At Greenstretch, we focus on measuring, preventing, limiting, and correcting environmental damage to water, air, and soil. We also address issues related to waste, noise, and ecosystems, offering a holistic approach to environmental solutions.

Our Services

Greenstretch Consultants Ltd offers a range of services and solutions that prioritize circularity, sustainability, and environmental compliance.

Environmental Consultancy

A comprehensive suite of consultancy services including air quality assessments, noise level measurements, environmental impact assessments, and more.

Waste and Recycling Solutions

Implementing waste reduction strategies, waste sorting, materials recovery, and offering waste management plans for a sustainable future.

Health and Safety Services

Providing health and safety training, risk assessments, emergency response and preparedness plans, and policy development for a secure working environment.

Products and Other Services
